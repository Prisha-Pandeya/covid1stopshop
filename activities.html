ACTIVITIES

The best thing to do for the pandemic to go away faster and to help keep you and others safe is to stay at home. But there is only so much you can do at home. Here are a list of activities you can do while being safe.

Redo You Home

Bored of the same old walls and style of your home? Change it up and make something new! It eats some time and yields a great output, and its really fun!

Learn A New Insturment

Whether you already play an instument or you always wanted to learn, the pandemic brought a great time to star playing an insturment and improve on it!

Get A New Hobby

Pick up a new hobby while your stuck at home. Its a great thing to do, and who knows, you may even enjoy it! Whether its drawing, photography, a boardgame, or even juggling, its definetly worth trying.

Do Workouts

Physical fitness is very important for our mental and physical health. With gyms and PE classes closed down due to the pandemic, its important that we exercise routinely. You can find workouts on websites like darebee.com and video workouts on YouTube
