About

The recent SARS-CoV-2 pandemic, aka the COVID-19 pandemic, has completly changed our lives for the time being. There is a lot of information to keep in mind, and it's all very widespread. As things calm down, we see lots of different website, and the problem of confusion and unknowingness beings to arise.

The Covid-19 One Stop Shop changes that.

The Covid-19 One Stop Shops contains most of the informaiton one needs to understand the pandemic and its next steps. This website contains all the information related to Covid-19, and where you can deepen your understanding of it. Go explore our website to check out all the information!
